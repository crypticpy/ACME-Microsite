Analysis Methodology

Sentiment Analysis: Each theme's sentiment was determined through qualitative analysis of response tone, language patterns, and emotional indicators. Responses expressing frustration, concern, or criticism were classified as negative; those expressing hope, satisfaction, or support were positive; and mixed responses containing both elements were noted accordingly.

Priority Classification: Priority levels (Critical, High, Medium) were assigned based on three factors: frequency of mentions (volume), intensity of sentiment (urgency in language), and potential impact on the creative community's sustainability and equity. Themes affecting basic needs, access, or systemic barriers received higher priority ratings.
